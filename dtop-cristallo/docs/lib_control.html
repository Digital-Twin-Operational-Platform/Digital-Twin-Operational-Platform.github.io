Structural vibration control¶

This function calculates the kinetic energy of the original structure without control.

dtLib.vibcontrol.passiveStructure.keoriginal()¶

This function calculates the kinetic energy of the structure controlled by a passive tuned mass damper.

dtLib.vibcontrol.passiveControlTMD.keTMD(nfs, mb, mp, kp, cp)¶

This function calculates the kinetic energy of the structure controlled by a direct velocity feedback.

dtLib.vibcontrol.activeControlVFC.keVFC(nfs, mb, mp, kp, cp, Bl, Ze, h)¶

This function calculates the kinetic energy of the structure controlled by a direct velocity feedback in series with an electronic compensator.
